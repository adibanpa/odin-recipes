Pizza Recipe

Description

This recipe demonstrates how to easily make home made dough and sauce with very little effort. Credit cose to Adam Ragusea as this recipe was inspired by his collection of videos on pizza dough, pizza sauce, and general pizza recipes.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Crushed tomato
  • Italian seasoning
  • Garlic powder
  • Mozarrella cheese

Steps to make dough

  1. Mix 5 cups of flour, 1 tbsp of salt, 1 tsp of yeast, 1, and 1 tbsp of sugar.
  2. Add 2 cups of water, and 2 tbsp of olive oil.
  3. Mix into dough and let it rest.

Steps to make sauce

  1. Combine 28oz of crushed tomato, 2-4 tbsp of olive, 1 tsp of sugar, 2 tsp of italian seasoning.
  2. Mix until desired consistency

Steps to make pizza

  1. Turn oven on broiler setting
  2. Put olive oil in a a frying pan, add salt, pepper, italian seasoning, and garlic powder.
  3. Spread mixture on pan
  4. Spread dough on pan
  5. Cook dough until crispy
  6. Spread sauce, cheese, and basil (optional)
  7. Place in oven for 3-5 mins
  8. When brown, remove from oven and let it rest
